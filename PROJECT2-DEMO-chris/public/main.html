GarageSwap



BUY

At Garage-Swap, you'll find more than your neighbors'
outdated belongings. We store a sundry of locally-owned
items, ranging from modern appliances to more intimate wares,
like a crib for infants or even vintage jewelry. Peruse our inventory and discover the value of trading within your community, rather than with globalized corporations. Engage your neighbors via Garage-Swap you'll be able to shop for items at a good price, all from the safety and confort of your own home!

SELL

You'll also discover the values of a local market
by posting any items or your own that you may no longer need.
Repurposing personal affects is a great way to reduce our carbon footprint, as well as to engage with your neighbors, especially when we're encouraged to maintain a socially-responsible distance. So help the planet, and help your own community by posting those items that you no longer need. Happy swapping!
